## Option 1: Personal and Direct

Hi everyone,

I am excited to share something I have been building: **eMFer - Empirical Mutual Fund Evaluation & Research**.

eMFer is a mutual fund research platform that helps you look beyond simple 1 year, 3 year, and 5 year returns.

You can use it to:

- compare mutual funds side by side
- study rolling CAGR performance
- understand risk-return tradeoffs
- check SIP and lumpsum return outcomes
- ask Scout, an AI research assistant, to explain fund performance in simple language

I built this because I wanted mutual fund analysis to feel easier, clearer, and less overwhelming for everyday investors.

This is meant for research and learning only, not investment advice.
